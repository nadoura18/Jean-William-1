Consultancy

The Consultancy Section of our firm stands as the cornerstone of our client-centric approach, offering tailored solutions that transcend the ordinary. With a deep-rooted commitment to understanding our clients' visions, aspirations, and objectives, our consultancy services serve as the guiding force behind every successful event. We pride ourselves on cultivating collaborative partnerships, harnessing our expertise in event planning, design, and coordination to craft unique experiences that leave lasting impressions.

Consultancy for business modules

Consultancy in the events business involves providing expert guidance, support, and strategic advice to individuals or organizations involved in planning, organizing, and executing events.

Technical Consultancy in project installations and mega events

Technical consultancy in project installations and mega events involves providing specialized expertise and guidance in the planning, implementing, and managing of complex technical aspects within large-scale projects or events.

Consultancy for complex designs and assembly.

Consultancy for complex designs and assembly involves specialized guidance and expertise in handling intricate design projects that require meticulous planning, innovative solutions, and precise assembly processes.

Consultancy in media server and Networking

Consultancy in media servers and networking involves providing specialized expertise and guidance in setting up, configuring, and managing media servers and networking infrastructure.

Design

Discovery and Understanding

It begins with in-depth conversations and research to understand the client's vision, goals, audience, and preferences.

Conceptualization and Ideation

ased on the insights gained, the design team brainstorms ideas, themes, and concepts that will form the backbone of the event.

Design Development

Once a concept is agreed upon, the design team starts fleshing out the details. This includes selecting color schemes, creating floor plans, designing stage sets, choosing decorations, lighting, and considering audiovisual elements.

Execution Planning

This stage involves turning the finalized designs into actionable plans. It includes logistics planning, supplier coordination, technical setup, and detailing the timeline for the event day.
